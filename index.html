Jinyuan Shao 邵金圆

I am a PhD candidate at Purdue University advised by Prof. Songlin Fei. Prior to Purdue, I obtained my Master of Science from the Insitute of Urban Environment, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research interests include LiDAR, Remote Sensing, and Machine Learning. I'm currently focusing on advancing automatic forest inventory with LiDAR point clouds. By leveraging modern sensing technologies and intelligence algorithms, I hope to improve the traditional labor-intensive forest inventory so as to promote global sustainable forest practices.
